We take reservations for lunch and dinner. To make a reservation, please call us at (027) 8338 145 between 10am-6pm, Monday to Friday.

private dinings & events


Whatever your event or celebration, we are here to make your special day a truly memorable one. Renaissance’s private dining experts are more than happy to create custom floral arrangements and arrange for guests to meet Chef Moise during the course of their meal. For parties of 8-12 we have the roastery table which is available for private hire.
